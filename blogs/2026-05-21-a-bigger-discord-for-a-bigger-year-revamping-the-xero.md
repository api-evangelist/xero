You probably heard we’re bringing Xero Developer Days back in 2026 , this time, in two cities. London on 7 July (alongside Xerocon London on 8–9 July) and Denver on 18 August (alongside Xerocon Denver on 19–20 August). We’re also running a hackathon in London in the lead-up to DevDay.
