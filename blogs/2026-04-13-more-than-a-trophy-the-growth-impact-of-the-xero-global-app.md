<figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*T8Ms-UbQ47WAeQXG9iBf-Q.png" /></figure><p>While winning an award is a fantastic moment of celebration for any team, the data from our 2025 Global App Awards show that the impact goes far beyond the (metaphorical) trophy. Being recognised as a winner or finalist provides a significant boost to visibility and customer trust, directly translating into app growth.</p><h3>Celebrating the 2025 winners</h3><p>The 2025 Global App Awards showcased the best of the best across our ecosystem, from innovative industry-specific tools to the emerging stars of the year. You can view the<a href="https://devblog.xero.com/global-app-awards-2025-celebrating-our-winners-b137e16957f5"> <strong>full list of last year’s winners here.</strong></a></p><h3>The ‘award effect’</h3><p>We took a look at the performance data for our 2025 winners, and the results show a clear trend in increased adoption following the announcement.</p><p>This ‘award effect’ highlights how Xero’s marketing and brand can amplify your app’s reach. The surge in interest suggests that the ‘winner’ title acts as a powerful trust signal for small businesses and advisors looking for verified, high-quality solutions. When customers see an award-winning app, they see a verified, high-quality solution that has been vetted by the best in the industry.</p><blockquote>“Honestly, when we found out we’d won Global Practice App of the Year, the whole team just lit up. We’ve been heads-down building something we truly believe in, so to have Xero and the global accounting community recognize that means the world to us. And the visibility has been remarkable — we’re connecting with practices we never would have reached otherwise, which is exactly why we do this.”</blockquote><p><em>Andrey Kustarnikov, Founder and CEO, </em><a href="https://apps.xero.com/app/g-accon-for-xero"><em>G-Accon</em></a></p><h3>Why recognition matters for growth</h3><p>Winning an award gives you more than just a badge of honor; it provides a unique set of growth levers:</p><ul><li><strong>Targeted visibility:</strong> Finalists and winners are featured in Xero marketing initiatives, social media, and dedicated Xero App Store collections that are promoted.</li><li><strong>Internal motivation: </strong>Recognition from Xero is a powerful validation of your team’s hard work and commitment to the small business community.</li></ul><p><strong>How to get involved</strong></p><p>The Xero Global App Awards are an opportunity to showcase your innovation, your commitment to the Xero community, and the success of your customers.
